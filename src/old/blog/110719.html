thanks wave

wave.ac announced its closure as a web streaming plataform effective 10 of august, which is a shame, although I wasnt particularily active in its community, their sound quality and just their existence meant trusting it as a backup for my soundcloud, if anything happened to my account, the songs would still be wave.ac

The wavefest was a great time, and it lead me to meeting some pretty cool people!
The team did their job well, i understand their reasons, and i hope the best for them in the future
